# nvr

Ubiquiti UNVR Instant with a Western Digital Purple 4TB (WD43PURZ) surveillance-rated HDD. The UNVR is the local recording target for all 7 cameras (6x G5 Bullet + G4 Doorbell Pro) and lives on VLAN 10 with no internet access. This section will hold retention policy notes, SMART / disk health monitoring, backup procedures for recordings, and eventually a small app that surfaces free space, disk temperature, and recording gap alerts.

**Status:** Not yet started.
